Our Name:

Where did our name come from? Well our C.E.O. took our founding member's first names of course! Jacob (C.E.O.) Andrew, Julieth, Angela, and Kathryn are the original team and founding members of A.J.A.K. Not to be confused with AJAX, which is a completely unrelated development set for web applications.

We know what you're thinking,"Were they just incapable of coming up with their own name?" and no, we are self proclamed narcissists who enjoy the first letter of our first names and wanted to symbolize kinship in our company name by combining our name to represent ourselves in all our glory as one.

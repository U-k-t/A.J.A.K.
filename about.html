The A.J.A.K. About Page

Our mission at A.J.A.K.co is establishing personal projects with vigor, enthusiasm and energy. We offer a unique design experience for everyone, by providing the best custom sites and programs. At A.J.A.K we have dedication to the highest quality of custom sites and programs delivered efficiently, precisely, and adaptively. A.J.A.K. values teamwork and dedication to our craft. We plan our company will help change the world dramatically in making it a better place.

Our Name:

Where did our name come from? Well our C.E.O. took our founding member's first names of course! Jacob (C.E.O.) Andrew, Julieth, Angela, and Kathryn are the original team and founding members of A.J.A.K. Not to be confused with AJAX, which is a completely unrelated development set for web applications.

We know what you're thinking,"Were they just incapable of coming up with their own name?" and no, we are self proclamed narcissists who enjoy the first letter of our first names and wanted to symbolize kinship in our company name by combining our name to represent ourselves in all our glory as one.

Our Story:

The A.J.A.K. Company founders came together one wonderfully drunken night and wanted to be the change in the problems that they always complained about. Jacob's orginial plan was huge and ambitious but ultimately his decisions, and the other founding members' influence led to a happy accident known as the A.J.A.K. company. Now, we represent solidarity and commitment.
